Join the Odilia Community!

The Odilia dev team regularly hangs out in our Matrix space: #odilia-screen-reader:matrix.org. Whether you have plans to contribute, want to follow along with development, or just want some fellow nerds to chat to, come join us! We also bridge this space to discord, and you can get in touch with the developers directly if you wish.

Contributing

There are many ways you can contribute to Odilia.

  1. If you’re a developer, send us a pull-request. Help us make Odilia better by fixing an outstanding issue.
  2. If you’re a user, install Odilia, and report your bugs and feature requests to us on Github.
  3. If you believe in our cause, consider donating to one of us, as we continue to develop this project. It allows us to spend more time on Odilia, and less time at work.
