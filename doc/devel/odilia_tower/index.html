Integrations for the Odilia screen reader and the tower ecosystem. While most Tower crates focus on networking applications, we beleive that it has great usefulness to us as assistive technology makers.

Most of these are generic-ish utilities we’ve needed in Odilia. We are open to removing them in the case that general-purpose ones are avaialbe and easy to integrate.
